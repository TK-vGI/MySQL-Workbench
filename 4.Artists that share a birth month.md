# Stage 4/5:Artists that share a birth month
## Description
In the previous stage, you had some practice with aggregate functions and subquery. You will take things a nudge  
higher with self-join in this stage.

Self-joins allow you to join a table to itself, enabling comparisons between different rows within the same table.  
Understanding how self-joins work is an essential data manipulation skill. However, the concept of joining a table to  
itself can be abstract and difficult to grasp. Unlike traditional joins where tables are visually distinct, self-joins  
involve the same table being referenced multiple times, which can lead to confusion when trying to visualize  
the operation.

In this stage, you'll utilize a self-join on the artist table to compare the birth months of all artists. You will  
compare their birth months and list only artists that share birth months.
